# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want datapress to be a welcoming, respectful, and productive project for everyone. Contributors, maintainers, and users are expected to treat each other with professionalism and care.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful of differing experience levels, backgrounds, and viewpoints.
- Give and accept constructive feedback gracefully.
- Keep discussions focused on the project and its users.
- Assume good intent, and ask clarifying questions before escalating disagreement.
- Help make the project easier to understand, use, and contribute to.

## Unacceptable behavior

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discriminatory language, personal attacks, sustained disruption, publishing others' private information, or any conduct that would reasonably make someone feel unsafe or unwelcome.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, issues, pull requests, or other contributions that violate this code of conduct. Repeated or severe violations may lead to temporary or permanent restrictions from project spaces.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it privately using GitHub's security or contact mechanisms for this repository. Reports will be reviewed with care and confidentiality.
